# Pedagogical Reforms, Public Education Expenditure and Educational Performance in Côte d'Ivoire (1962–2025)

## Overview

This repository contains the data, econometric codes, estimation outputs, and supplementary materials used in the study:

**"Pedagogical Reforms, Public Education Expenditure and Educational Performance in Côte d'Ivoire (1962–2025)"**

The study investigates the effects of major pedagogical reforms and public education expenditure on educational performance in Côte d'Ivoire over the period 1962–2025. The empirical analysis combines ARDL-ECM and VAR approaches to identify both long-run relationships and dynamic effects across educational levels (CEPE, BEPC and BAC).

## Research Objectives

The study aims to:

* Assess the impact of pedagogical reforms on national examination performance;
* Evaluate the role of public education expenditure in educational outcomes;
* Examine short-run and long-run dynamics between educational policies and performance indicators;
* Provide empirical evidence on the effectiveness of educational reforms in Côte d'Ivoire.

## Methodology

The analysis relies on:

* Descriptive statistics and correlation analysis;
* Unit root testing (ADF, PP, ERS, Ng-Perron, KPSS);
* ARDL Bounds Testing and Error Correction Modelling (ECM);
* Vector Autoregressive (VAR) models;
* Granger causality tests;
* Impulse Response Functions (IRFs);
* Forecast Error Variance Decomposition (FEVD);
* Robustness and diagnostic tests.

## Data Sources

Data were compiled from:

* Ministry of National Education of Côte d'Ivoire;
* Institute of Developing Countries
* World Bank World Development Indicators (WDI);
* Historical administrative and educational records.

## Reproducibility

This repository is intended to ensure full transparency and reproducibility of the empirical results reported in the article. All estimation procedures, datasets, and supplementary materials necessary to replicate the findings are provided.
